**Project idea**

- Idea: Tied to Energy 

**What is the problem you are solving?**

-The Problem: High energy costs and grid reliability. Solving the "uncertainty" of when and where switching to solar actually pays off.

**What tools did you use?**

-Tools:  using Python with GeoPandas (for county mapping) and Pandas (for financial modeling) 

**What insights did you or do you want to discover?/ What Solutions do you want to offer? Do People even need these solutions?**

-Insights:"break-even" point—the exact moment solar becomes cheaper than the grid in specific regions.

**How would a business or a community (for Social Impact Projects) benefit from your work?**

-Impact: Real financial savings. A business can see exactly how much their ROI improves by switching, leading to better long-term capital decisions.
